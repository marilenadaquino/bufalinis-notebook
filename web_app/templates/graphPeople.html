Index of Persons

Graph

In the chart below all the persons mentioned in the notebook are represented as nodes. The relations between them, on the other hand, correspond to the arcs of the graph. By hovering on each node, a tooltip shows the name of the corresponding cited person. If that person mentions other persons, the arcs that connect that person to all the persons she mentions are highlighted. By analyzing the arcs that connect the nodes, the user can discover the network of citations that characterizes Bufalini's text. Furthermore, from the proximity between different nodes we can deduce the interests in common to different persons (e.g. Tacito and Giussani are very close nodes and both are connected to persons belonging to the Latin world).
