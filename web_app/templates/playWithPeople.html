Index of Persons

Play with data

The chart below is designed as an environment in which users can explore the data in a funny way and discover new relationships between them.
Each person who mentions other persons is represented as a circle with a white margin, while persons who are mentioned from that same person as smaller circles with no margin. By hovering on each circle, the name of the person associated with the circle and the number of excerpts in which she is cited are displayed.
The size of the circles is proportional to the number of extracts in which the person is mentioned.
Persons who are mentioned can be mentioned by several persons at the same time. By exploring the chart, the user can search for how many nodes are repeated, discovering who mentions the person searched.
To facilitate the search, each node with a margin can be opened and closed. By closing all the nodes, the user will find out who are all the persons who mention other persons in the notebook.
